Julio Duvergé

I always considered myself an out-of-the-box thinker, since a young age, I always looked for different answers for the same questions, and I think that this mentality carried my personal and professional life on becoming what I am today.

That frame of thinking mixed with my longtime passion for all type of multimedia, art and entertainment thanks to an early exposure to creative platforms like MTV, VH1 where I experienced, music, art, fashion and avant-garde ideas that lately put me in the place to work with Nucleo Media for the role of filmmaker and editor, in this agency we collaborated with multinational companies like Pernod Ricard, Tabacalera Garcia, and local companies like Avelino Abreu, Banco Caribe y Santo Domingo Motors.

Later in search of experience in my area, I studied in UNAPEC looking for an environment where I could develop new relationship with fellow like-minded people and learn the best practices to increase the visibility and the performances of brands, products and people.

That education lead me to work with the Center for Agricultural and Forestry Development in the sustainability department Sostenibilidad 3Rs, in where my tasks involved developing the online and offline presence of the department and consequently increase the demand for environmental consulting services. Adding to that I was the manager of the social/environmental non-profit project Vecino Verde who was targeted to business and residences that were interested in recycle and helping people of low income resources.

In my most recent experience I worked with the international NGO Oxfam International in a consulting service in which I created a strategic marketing plan to develop entrepreneurships in the region of Dajabón, we targeted the honey based product company AMAMIEL, leaded by a team of women that live in the Northwest region of the country.

At this moment I'm looking to keep expanding my competitions and my professional career, targeting now a focus on Data Analytics, Business Analysis and Data Science.
